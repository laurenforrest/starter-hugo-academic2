Junie (aka Junebug) is a 2ish-year-old lovebug with the sweetest personality. While not much is known about her life before rescue, she arrived at her foster family's home with her four adolescent kittens after proving what a devoted mama she was.

Now it's Junie's turn to receive the care she deserves. Her favorite place is right by her human's side, and she'll happily follow you from room to room to make sure you never feel alone. She's also very punctual when it comes to mealtime and will be sure to remind you if dinner is even a minute late! She is playful, gentle, affectionate, and cuddly.

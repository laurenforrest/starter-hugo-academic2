Mama is a 3-year-old sweetheart with so much love to give! She was originally found on the streets of Miami caring for her five kittens. After spending some time with her foster mom and raising her babies, she found her forever home with her human.

Mama has always been very sweet and affectionate, but once her motherly duties were completed, her true personality really began to shine. She's blossomed into the playful and fun-loving girl she was always meant to be. You can usually find her following around her human or sleeping in the sun in a silly position.
